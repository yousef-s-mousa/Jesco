Strawberries
products


Strawberries are among the fruits which change its colors several times during its life time. To start with the fruits are green and then change to white and then change its color partially into rose and then to red color. This is accompanied with an increase in fruits size and an increase in the rate of humidity with an increase in the sugary materials which form 70% - 80% of the soluble solid materials.
